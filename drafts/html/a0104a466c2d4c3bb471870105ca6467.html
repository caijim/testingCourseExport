The timed exams feature allows an instructor to set a time limit for each problem in an assignment. A student cannot see a timed problem until they click the "Start Timer" button, at which point they are given a certain amount of time to click the Submit button or receive no points for that question. A countdown timer lets them know how much time remains. Once the timer reaches zero, the student is no longer allowed to submit their answer. The timer continues counting down even if they leave the page or lose their internet connection.

To turn a problem into a timed problem, go to the Edit page of the component and put a non-zero value in the "Minutes Allowed" field.
