NAVIGATING THE SITE

The left-hand column of this page is like a Table of Contents for the course. It looks something like this:

Click on a section to expand it, then begin a lesson by clicking the black text.

Each lesson consists of multiple elements, possibly including things like readings, videos, and assessments. These are displayed on a timeline, which is the bar you see at the top of this page. It looks like this:

The page highlighted in white is the one you're on now. You can skip forward or backward by clicking on the icons in the bar. You can also go use the black arrows at either end of the timeline. The same black arrows are found at the bottom of the page. They look like this:

If you find that you cannot continue forward, it means you have reached the end of the lesson and need to choose the next one from the list on the left.

When you need a break, you can stop and return to finish the lesson at a later time. If you close the browser window, the system will remember where you were last time and ask if you want to continue from that point in the lesson. (It won't restart you from the beginning of the lesson.)

There are many other resources and pages in the course site, so if you find yourself lost, you can always return to this view by clicking the Courseware link at the top left of the page.

This is the end of the navigation section. Click "Videos and Problems" from the menu on the left to go on to the next part of the introduction.
